MAIN ACTIVITIES

BUILDING INFORMATION MODELING (BIM)

BIM stands for Building Information Modeling and is a workflow process. It's based around models used for the planning, design, construction, and management of building and infrastructure projects. BIM software is used to model and optimize projects by planning, designing, building, and operating BIM models.
The most capable software to manage different phases in architecture is REVIT. Because a building can be model in construction, architecture, and energy efficiency and make a relation among all of them. Despite of professional experience, I had a course of BIM in renovation project with Pr. Bruno Daniotti.

BUILDING energy EFFICIENCY

Building Energy Modeling (BEM) is a multi-purpose tool for building energy efficiency, supporting projects at the level of individual buildings (design, control, rating, financial incentives) and building stocks (program development, research).
From building physics to BIM, my university projects were focused on how to improve energy efficiency of the building , which include both passive and active strategies. Finally, in Adaptive façade we designed a dynamic façade which scheduled with climate changes and user needs.
I mainly do my projects with IESVE and perform them with Excel. While in adaptive façade, the building was modelled with grasshopper, and I got the results with Ladybug and Honeybee.
