RENDER & POST PRODUCTION

After producing raw images from render motor, post-production is an essential process to improve the quality of image and prepare it for presentation. This process adds the final touches to the imagery. At this stage, one can combine a render with photos, adjust its visual properties, and add special effects.

RENDER & POST PRODUCTION SKILLS

Photorealistic ray traced rendering

V-Ray renderer uses adaptive ray tracing technology and proprietary scene intelligence to create photorealistic imagery and animations. It accurately calculates the distribution of light as well as the physical properties of any material.
In addition to high quality renders were prepared with this render motor, it can be added to 3Ds-max. Therefore, I select it to prepare raw renders for post production.

Post-production Process

Post-production is the process of editing and adding effects to photos and video footage.
V-ray motor render engine provide separated layers of light calculation and material effect of the final image. Therefore, we can decrease or increase different layers effects to get a better result. In addition, PNG photos of real objects improve the visual reality of the final render. .
