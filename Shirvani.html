SHIRVANI RESIDENTIAL COMPLEX

The project concerns design of 3 floor building with an area of 1000 m2 . Each floor consists of a unit with a large balcony . In the design, using the climatic architecture of the area, a shell with a sloping roof covered the entire project .By creating vertical openings on the sides of the project, we created an air stream . In the interior design that impressed us most of all, it was a very beautiful view that surrounded nature ,So we used great openings .In order to coordinate with the surrounding nature, we tried to bring nature into the house using wood material. As requested by the client, the project ceiling was used as a public gathering space
